Information

This project tracks aerial and border-related incidents across Europe, including drone sightings, airspace incursions, balloon events, and suspected hybrid activities.

What is counted as an incident?

An incident represents a single event or operational episode. If multiple sightings are part of the same event (for example, the same drone observed in multiple locations), they may be grouped together.

Confirmation status

Incidents are included regardless of confirmation level. Visual markers and colors indicate whether attribution is confirmed, suspected, unknown, or unverified.

Some contextual details (e.g. military usage of bases) are included without explicit sourcing when they are widely accepted facts. Attribution claims are always sourced.

Sources

Sources include national media, public broadcasters, international news agencies, and official airport or government statements.
