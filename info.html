What counts as an incident?

An incident is defined as any event that involves an airspace or border violation, drone or balloon sightings near sensitive areas, or unusual military or reconnaissance activity. Only events verified by reliable sources are counted.

Why not everything is updated

Not all events are immediately added to the calendar or map. Some reports take time to verify, some are low-risk and not considered notable, and some details may be confidential or unconfirmed. We prioritize accuracy over speed.

Data sources

All incidents are based on publicly available sources, news outlets, and official military or civil reports. Links to source articles are provided when possible.
