This page explains how incidents are reported and tracked. Here you can learn more about the color coding, icons, and reporting process.

Incident Reporting

Incidents are categorized by type (drone, jet, balloon, border) and location. Each incident entry includes:

  • Country and city/region
  • Date and time
  • Type of incident
  • Details and links to sources
  • Notes on confirmation or suspected actor

Legend / Colors

Colors indicate incident count and actor verification:

Notes

All incidents are verified as much as possible with publicly available sources. Times are local to the incident location.
